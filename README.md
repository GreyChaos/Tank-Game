# Tank Game 
### V.0.10 Networking Part 2!
##

A multiplayer top down tank battle game built in Godot. Drive around chaotic arenas, shoot your friends (or CPUs), get powerups, and survive hazards like cracking ice and raining nuke. Last tank standing wins.

## Features

**Local multiplayer** Host a server or join over IP (port 9810)

**AI-controlled CPUs** Fill empty slots with bots that pathfind toward enemies and objectives

**5 maps** with a random map rotation between rounds (the last played map is excluded from the next pick)

 **Two game modes:**
  - **Free-For-All (FFA)** Last tank standing wins
  - **Capture the Flag (CTF)** Steal the enemy flag and bring it home

 **Powerups** that change your tank's stats or next shot:
  - Health boost
  - Nuke (rains 25 explosions across the map)
  - Big Shot (a larger, faster/slower shell that goes through objects)
  - Triple Shot
  - 360° Shot (a full ring of shells)

 **Map hazards:**
  - Cracking ice tiles that eventually break and deal heavy damage
  - Falling nuke strikes
  - A spinning sand pit that flips your tank's direction
  - Cacti that damage on contact

 **Customization**  pick your name and tank color, with settings saved between sessions

 **Fun Hats** winners get a hat for the next match

 **Persistent settings** for player name, color, last connected server, volume, and fullscreen preference


## Controls

WASD for movement, and SPACE to fire your next shot

ESC to leave the current game, and return to the main menu
